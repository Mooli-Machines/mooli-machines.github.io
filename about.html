The mooli, which you might know as the daikon, is a humble radish which is often used as a cover crop.

Once planted, it works quitely and diligently, breaking up rough soil, preventing weeds from taking root, and delivering and maintaining nutrients.

It also happens to be tasty.


Mooli Machines wishes to be a radish for your business. Working with Mooli means prepping the fields for whatever you want to grow down the line.

We love to work with small-to-medium-sized businesses or teams within larger businesses to tackle problems with data, integration, and automation.

We work with your existing technology and processes and slowly break up that rough soil, growing with you and making small changes to prevent weeds from taking root.

In time, the soil will be rich and nutritious, and you will be ready to farm.


Our long term goal is to produce public goods in the form of research and tools which will help bring automation to the masses.
